MOI CCTV Readiness Checklist (Doha)

1) Site survey completed with area map and camera points.
2) Sector requirements reviewed and translated into design scope.
3) Camera schedule prepared (model, lens, location, use-case).
4) Storage retention estimate completed and documented.
5) Network and PoE capacity plan finalized.
6) NVR/VMS architecture and user permission matrix prepared.
7) Cabling, rack, and power backup plan confirmed.
8) Commissioning and test checklist approved.
9) Preventive maintenance and SLA response scope defined.
10) Handover pack prepared (as-built docs, training, and support contacts).
